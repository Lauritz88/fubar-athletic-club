# FUBAR Athletic Club — Project Context

## Brand
FUBAR is a premium technical running and training apparel brand from Grimstad, Norway. The tagline is "Beyond Recovery". The brand operates on limited drops — when sold out, it's gone. Target audience is serious runners and strength athletes (25-40) who value quality and community over mainstream brands.

## Design Principles
- Minimal, clean, premium — like Satisfy Running, District Vision, or Tracksmith
- Ultra-subtle branding — tonal prints, small embroidery, no big logos
- Dark aesthetic: deep charcoal, forest green, off-white
- Editorial photography style — moody, authentic, not staged
- Scandinavian industrial feel — concrete, dawn light, raw environments
- High-end Framer-like motion: smooth scroll animations, parallax, staggered reveals

## Tech Stack
- **Framework:** Next.js 15 (App Router) with TypeScript
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS v4 with custom CSS variables
- **Animation:** Motion (formerly Framer Motion) for React animations
- **Fonts:** Space Mono (display/accent), DM Sans (body)
- **Hosting:** Vercel (deploy from GitHub)
- **E-commerce (Phase 2):** Shopify Storefront API, Stripe, or Lemon Squeezy

## Color Palette
- `--asfalt`: #0A0A0A (primary dark)
- `--grus`: #1A1A1A (secondary dark)
- `--ben`: #E8E4DF (off-white / bone)
- `--rust`: #C4653A (accent, use sparingly)
- `--skog`: #2D4A3E (forest green)

## Typography Rules
- Display: Space Mono, 400 weight, wide tracking (8-16px letter-spacing), all caps
- Body: DM Sans, 300-400 weight, generous line-height (1.7-2)
- Never use Inter, Arial, or default system fonts

## Voice & Copy
- Direct, honest, no marketing fluff
- Norwegian and English both work
- Focus on the "why" not the "what" — emotional, not technical
- "Beyond Recovery" means pushing past comfort, not just physical recovery

## Do NOT
- Use purple gradients, neon colors, or AI-slop aesthetics
- Add large logos or loud branding anywhere
- Use Inter or other generic fonts
- Write long product descriptions — let images and spec sheets do the work
- Mention founder personal info (Stian) — FUBAR stands alone as a brand
